About IICKC

The India International Chamber of Knowledge and Commerce (IICKC) connects Indian businesses and institutions with global partners through knowledge, advocacy, and on‑ground facilitation.

Mission & Vision: To strengthen India's international trade capacity by delivering market intelligence, fostering partnerships, and supporting business growth through high-quality programs and policy advocacy.
