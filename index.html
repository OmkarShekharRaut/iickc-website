About IICKC

The India International Chamber of Knowledge and Commerce (IICKC) connects Indian businesses and institutions with global partners through networking, advocacy, and on‚Äëground facilitation.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst for global growth and innovation by empowering Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) through international partnerships, knowledge exchange, and sector-focused strategies, fostering sustainable trade, commerce, and cultural integration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enable Indian Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to thrive globally by:

  • Facilitating international trade, technology partnerships, and joint ventures
  • Organizing impactful trade delegations and B2B forums across key global markets
  • Collaborating with global chambers, trade bodies, and institutions to build enduring commercial and cultural ties
  • Providing expert guidance to help enterprises leverage government schemes and export incentives
  • Delivering cutting-edge knowledge-sharing platforms across diverse high-impact sectors including Engineering, Manufacturing, Construction, IT & ITES, Pharmaceutical, Agriculture, Defence & Space Technology
