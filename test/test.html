Export Documentation Required for Exporting Goods

Exporting goods from India requires compliance with customs, regulatory, and destination country requirements. The following checklist supports MSMEs and businesses to prepare necessary documentation.

1. Mandatory Documents

Document Issued By Purpose Mandatory / Conditional
Import Export Code (IEC) Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) Authorization to export goods Mandatory
Shipping Bill / Bill of Export Indian Customs (ICEGATE) Primary document for customs clearance Mandatory
Commercial Invoice Exporter Declares product details, value, Incoterms Mandatory
Packing List Exporter Details on packages, weight, description Mandatory
Bill of Lading (Sea) / Airway Bill (Air) Shipping Line / Airline Proof of shipment and transport contract Mandatory
Export Declaration Form (EDF) Exporter / RBI via AD Bank Compliance with FEMA and RBI Mandatory

2. Financial & Regulatory Documents

Document Issued By Purpose Mandatory / Conditional
Letter of Credit (LC) / Bank Realisation Certificate (BRC) Importer’s/Exporter’s Bank Ensures payment compliance and forex regulations Conditional
Foreign Inward Remittance Certificate (FIRC) Exporter’s Bank Proof of receipt of foreign currency Conditional
Export License DGFT For restricted/prohibited goods Conditional

3. Certificates Based on Product and Destination

Document Issued By Required For Notes
Certificate of Origin (CoO) Chamber of Commerce / Export Promotion Council Many countries for duty benefits Under Free Trade Agreements
GSP Certificate Export Inspection Agency Duty benefits for exports to developed nations (EU, UK, etc.)
Health / Sanitary / Phytosanitary Certificate Government Authority For food, agriculture, dairy, meat, plants
Fumigation Certificate Plant Quarantine Authority For wood and agro-products
Inspection Certificate Export Inspection Agency (EIA) Quality and safety verification
Drug / Cosmetic License Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) For medicines, cosmetics
Halal / Kosher Certification Approved Certification Bodies Food exports to Middle East and Jewish markets
CE / UL / ISO Certification Standards Bodies Electronics, engineering, industrial goods

4. Region-Specific Documentation

  • European Union: CE Marking, REACH Compliance, GSP Certificate.
  • USA: FDA Approval for food/pharma; UL Certification for electronics; USDA permits.
  • Middle East (GCC): Halal Certificate, SASO Certificate (Saudi), Conformity Certificates.
  • Africa: Pre-shipment Inspection (PSI), SONCAP (Nigeria), Certificate of Origin (COO).
  • ASEAN Countries: Certificate of Origin (Form AI), Product Safety Certificates.
  • Australia/New Zealand: Biosecurity and Quarantine Clearances.

5. Post-Shipment Documentation

Document Issued By Purpose
Bank Realisation Certificate (BRC) Exporter’s Bank Proof of realization of export proceeds
Foreign Inward Remittance Certificate (FIRC) Exporter’s Bank Proof of foreign exchange inflow
GST Refund / Rebate Documents GST Network / Customs Claiming export incentives
Shipping Line Delivery Order Shipping Line Allows release of goods at destination port
